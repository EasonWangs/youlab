On December 26, 2023, Dr. Zhao Yunxiu's opening report "Microscale Optical Characterization of Chiral Biomolecules" was highly praised by the expert group at the review meeting and passed smoothly!

Dr. Zhao's report focused on the study of microscale optical properties of biochiral molecules. She creatively proposed the research idea of applying advanced optical characterization technology to biochiral systems. As a core member of the laboratory's "Chirality Exploration Team", Dr. Zhao has shown unique insights in the study of biomolecular spin selection, and her innovative research plan has made the experts present nod frequently.

It is reported that this research is expected to provide new characterization methods and theoretical basis for biochirality recognition. Let us look forward to Dr. Zhao's breakthrough progress in future research!
