On July 9, 2024, postdoctoral fellow Ni Yuqing joined our laboratory. Ni Yuqing is from Jinhua, Zhejiang, and graduated with a Ph.D. from Nanjing University of Technology. He has a solid research background in molecular dynamics post-processing analysis and two-dimensional infrared spectroscopy calculation. The addition of Dr. Ni Yuqing will further enhance the research strength of the team in theoretical spectral simulation and molecular dynamics. We look forward to his breakthrough achievements at Westlake University!
