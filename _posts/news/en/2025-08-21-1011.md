On August 1, 2025, the lab welcomed Tingting Ren as a new research assistant! A graduate of Yanbian University with a Master’s degree in Agronomy, Tingting will be primarily responsible for the theoretical introduction and operational use of the two-dimensional infrared spectroscopy (2DIR) instrument in our research group. With her strong multidisciplinary background and professional skills, she will bring fresh energy to the team’s research efforts. We believe her addition will infuse the lab with vibrant dynamism and innovative drive, and we look forward to witnessing her excel both in research and daily life!&nbsp;&nbsp;

Once again, a warm welcome to Tingting as she becomes part of our big family!
